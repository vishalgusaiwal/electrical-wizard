Signals

         it is a function that represents the variation of any physical quantity with respect to any parameter

         In electrical & electronics, usually signal is variation of electrical quantity with time

Transducer

         it changes non-electrical signals to electrical signals. Ex. mic-convert sound energy to electrical energy

Reverse-transducer

         it converts non-electrical signal to electrical signal. Ex. speaker-convert electrical energy to sound energy

Descrete Time-signal
